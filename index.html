Welcome to my Online Hub

This website is meant to be a culmination of my creative endeavors. Showcasing my ongoing journey in computer programming, visual art, and writing. I like to learn things and this is my little corner to share them with you.

A little about me...

I’m Efa - an aspiring computer programmer with an interest in Frontend Development.

My background is in Financial Services - Wealth Management and I’ve had previous lives in Healthcare Administration and IT. I tend to want to balance my analytical side with the creative by freelancing in graphic design, drawing and painting. Perhaps this is why programming is so appealing to me: it offers a healthy mix of both the “left and right brain”.

I am currently pursing a FullStack Certificate in Coding with MERN from MITxPro and am looking forward to building my skills throughout the program and beyond. This site is both a personal project and a means to share my learning. You can connect with me through LinkedIn, GitHub, or Email below.
